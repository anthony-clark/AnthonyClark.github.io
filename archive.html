A Snapshot Of Some Of The Designs And Pitch Work I Have Created Over The Years.

Visit Guernsey

Along with Atomic Design, working from a mobile first approach was also essential. Dealing with the smaller screen first meant the harder layout and design problems were dealt with earlier and allowed for a less problematic design phase. This was a massive time saver when it came to templates such as match centre, fixtures and player profiles.

Chester Races

My team and I worked collaboratively to produce designs for the website of Chester Races. My aim was to design a website that showcased the atmosphere of  the races but also allowed users, especially new visitors, to find out essential information and ticket availability.

Revolution Vodka Bars

I was lead designer and frontend developer on Revolution for around 6 years. In that time I worked very closely with Rev on projects ranging from new menus, student and Christmas campaigns, to creating a social competition where people could submit their choice of vodka flavour, getting friends to vote for it.

Predictor King

Flexible modules were designed for numerous elements such as player profiles. This saved having a number of different modules just to show a player in different pages/slots. Instead I designed one flexible module that could be tweaked using CSS to cater for a number of different scenarios. The same idea was used for features like next/last match modules and promotion modules. This, along with the other processes, led to a more streamlined design and development process.

Liverpool Airport

This project was essentially a re-skin. I had to add design to their current website in order to  achieve a more modern, vibrant look for a previously tired website.

Manchester Arena

I led the design and build on Manchester Arena website for 7 years. After the website went live I regularly created splash banners for events they wanted to promote each week.

Cotswold Council

Working closely with Cotswold Council, I created their new website by developing wireframes that met theirs and their users' needs before creating a striking, modern design.

Bolton Whites Hotel

I worked on a number of microsites for Bolton Wanderers including the website for the hotel that is within their stadium. They required a website that would showcase their bedrooms and hospitality lounges as well as allowing users to easily book a room or plan an event.
