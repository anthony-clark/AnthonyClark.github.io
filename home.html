More about me

I'm an Interaction designer with a passion for illustration based in Liverpool, UK

I have over 20 years of experience working with a vast number of large scale clients from a range of industries including Premier League football clubs, airports and arenas, tourism boards, restaurants and many more.

Currently, I am the Lead UI Designer at Barclays working on internal facing websites and apps, where I manage the team's design system and UI output. In this role, I am dedicated to making sure we are producing visually stunning, user-centric and accessible work that exceeds the expectations of our colleagues. I'm extremely hard-working, forward-thinking and demanding of myself. My passion is to create great work for clients which is visually the best in it's field and allows maximum user satisfaction.
