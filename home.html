About

UI/UX Designer with a passion for Illustration, Liverpool Football Club, Hiking and anything Star Wars.

I have over 17 years of experience working with a vast number of large scale clients from a range of industries including Premier League football clubs, airports and arenas, tourism boards, restaurants and many more.

Throughout my career, I have successfully managed teams of designers and developed strong skills in leading them towards successful outcomes by providing regular feedback, highlighting areas of strength and areas where they could improve. I encourage them to take on challenging projects, and I provide guidance and support throughout the process. By giving them autonomy and responsibility, I help them build confidence and develop their design skills which in turn leads to a strong cohesive design team.

Currently, I am the Lead UI Designer at Barclays working on internal facing websites and apps, where I manage the team's design system and UI output. In this role, I am dedicated to making sure we are producing visually stunning, user-centric and accessible work that exceeds the expectations of our colleagues.
