A Little Bit About Me...

Head Designer at Rippleffect (now Reading Room) with a passion for Illustration and Animation. I have 17 years' experience working with a large number of clients from a range of industries including Premier League football clubs, airports and arenas, tourism boards, restaurants and many more. For over 10 of these years I successfully managed a team of designers, dealing with Rippleffect's largest clients.

'm extremely hard-working, forward-thinking and demanding of myself. My passion is to create great work for clients which is visually the best in it's field and allows maximum user satisfaction.
